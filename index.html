TOP 10 BEST CAR BLOGS OF 2022

Are you obsessed with all things cars? You probably want to stay on top of the latest auto news in 2020 when it comes to the auto industry. The thing is, it can be difficult to keep up as the top trends .

Lucky for you, there are plenty of car blogs out there that solely focus on the auto industry. They provide everything from the best maintenance practices to thorough buying guides.

Are you a mechanic? Car enthusiast? Dealer? Regardless of how you're involved in the auto industry, you're bound to benefit from these top 10 best car blogs.


1.CAR GURUS



A popular go-to for many enthusiasts, this blog has it all. Its writers cover everything from newer models to classic and vintage vehicles. The blog also has in-depth video reviews on the latest models. This makes it a great resource for those looking for immersive content. Perhaps the best part about this blog is its dedicated staff. 2. KELLEY BLUE BOOK



Even if you aren't into cars, you've probably heard of Kelley Blue Book. This company is perhaps one of the most recognized names in the industry. However, you might not be aware of KBB's online presence. Users recognize its site as a comprehensive guide for buying and selling vehicles. Buyers and dealers alike trust this resource for their purchases. If you are buying or selling a vehicle, KBB is your go-to. You can get all the information you need about your make and model. This will help ensure you are well-informed and get a fair price.


3. EDMUNDS



Yet another big name in the industry, Edmunds is a go-to if you're buying or selling. The company has really grown its online presence in the past few years. Users depend on its comparison guides to make sound buying decisions. So, if you're stuck between two vehicles, you can reference the site's model-by-model comparisons.

Experts advise that you check sites like these before buying a vehicle. This is one of the most dependable sources, meaning you can be assured you're getting a good deal.

On this blog, you'll also find a variety of other topics. Its writers cover everything from leasing advice to what to look for when purchasing an SUV.

This blog showcases the year's newest vehicles. You can find information on your favorite models and thorough comparison guides.

The blog is also a great resource if you're looking to invest in instruments. Contributors thoroughly test different brands, allowing you to see which ones work best and are worth buying.

6. AUTO TRADER



Thorough research is the name of the game at Auto Trader. Its writers produce content that users of all experiences can benefit from. Newbies often use the guides on what to look for when buying new versus used vehicles. More extreme enthusiasts can find information on the biggest car shows across the country.


7. CARS



This blog is perhaps best known for its side-by-side comparisons. You can compare information for up to four models at a time. This allows you to become an informed buyer and get the best price. Users looking for information on both used and new vehicles trust this resource. On this site, you can get an overview of the most recent:

SUVs (compact, sub-compact, mid-size, and full-size), Sedans (compact, sub-compact, mid-size, and full-size), Sports cars, Wagons/hatchbacks, Pickup trucks, and Hybrids/plug-ins.

Here, you'll also find buying guides and details about auto shows around the country.


8. AUTO BLOG



Auto Blog prides itself on its wide array of tools. Users looking to make just about any buying decision can turn to this resource. Like with other blogs, it has comparison guides and owner reviews. It also features auto loan calculators to ensure you make financially grounded purchases.


9. CAR MAX



This well-known dealer has put together one of the top car blogs out there. Of course, you can find information on your make and model. This allows you to compare it to other vehicles you have your eye on.

Its blog also has information on the latest shopping trends. Because the company is an actual dealer, it can offer a unique perspective that you aren't likely to find elsewhere. You get an insider's glance at the current state of the auto industry and how it changes over time.


10. TOP SPEED



This last blog is one that people in the industry are crazy about. It is updated every month to ensure its content is always fresh and accurate. Its writers discuss everything from custom vehicles in the entertainment industry to the most popular auto shows. You can even find "spy shots" of makes that haven't been put out yet.
